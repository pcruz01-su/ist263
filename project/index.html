Welcome to Fundacion Adelante Guatemala

Fundacion Adelante Guatemala is a non-profit organization dedicated to making a positive impact in Guatemala. Our mission is to support communities, promote education, and improve the lives of Guatemalan people.

Discover the Beauty of Guatemala

Guatemala is a country of rich cultural heritage, stunning landscapes, and warm-hearted people. From the lush rainforests to the ancient Mayan ruins, there is so much to explore and appreciate.

Our Commitment

With your support, we aim to create lasting change in Guatemala. Together, we can build a brighter future for this beautiful nation and its people.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to empower and uplift the communities of Guatemala by providing access to education, healthcare, and sustainable resources. We believe in the potential of every individual, and through our collaborative efforts, we strive to create a better tomorrow for all.

Statistics
